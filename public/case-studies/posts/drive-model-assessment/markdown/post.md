# DRIVE Model Assessment ‚Äî Nervous System Rewiring in Real Time

This case study was a result of us validating our own in-house DRIVE Model for The Resonance Core Framework book we are writing for winter 2026. This combines our various topics of self-awareness and presence and applying it to creating what we call DRIVEs: Units of action that allow a person to embody their values and virtues into mini-applications in their everyday lives.

**This is not theory. This is lived data.**

## Understanding the DRIVE Model

The **DRIVE Model‚Ñ¢** is a six-stage cyclical framework designed to transform identity through micro-actions. Unlike traditional goal-setting or habit formation, the DRIVE Model treats each action as a **nervous system rewiring opportunity** ‚Äî tracking somatic, emotional, and narrative signals to distinguish between inherited fear patterns and genuine misalignment.

### The Six Stages of Identity Generation

The model operates as a complete loop, where each stage builds momentum toward identity evolution:

**1. SPARK ‚Üí RESONANCE** (The Ignition Point)  
**Inputs**: Identity Clarity, Values, Emotional Resonance  
**Output**: "This Matters"; Widening Window of Choice

This is where value/virtue pairs create the initial spark. The client identifies what truly matters and why.

**2. ALIGNMENT ‚Üí COHERENCE** (The Stabilizing Layer)  
**Inputs**: Triad Scoring, Guardrails, Journaling Signals  
**Output**: Grounded Direction, No Contradictions

The MVD (Minimally Viable DRIVE) is tested against the body's wisdom. Coherence ensures the action aligns with values, not survival patterns.

**3. ACTIVATION ‚Üí MICRO-ACTION** (The Biggest Step)  
**Inputs**: Single DRIVE, 1% Action, Ritual/Cue  
**Output**: Shift to Embodiment, Resistance Decreases

The action is taken. This is where the nervous system reveals its true response ‚Äî fear dissolves into flow or resistance reveals misalignment.

**4. MOMENTUM ‚Üí COMPOUNDING IDENTITY** (Action Becomes Self-Reinforcing)  
**Inputs**: Daily Journaling, DRIVE Scoring, Triad Recalibration  
**Output**: Increased Competence, Autonomy, Resonance, Self-Trust

Repeated micro-activations compound into new identity. The nervous system begins to trust the process.

**5. ENDURANCE ‚Üí SUSTAINABLE FUEL** (Where Motivation Becomes Irrelevant)  
**Inputs**: Consistency, Rituals, Environmental Design, Emotional Literacy  
**Output**: Long-Term Endurance, Stable Execution, Resilient DII (Desire-Integrity-Impact)

The action becomes automatic. Motivation is replaced by identity: "This is who I am."

**6. EXPANSION ‚Üí IDENTITY EVOLUTION** (The System Grows With You)  
**Inputs**: Reflection, Recalibration, New DRIVEs, Updated Guardrails  
**Output**: Evolving Identity, Wider Window of Choice, Resonant Life

The client expands into new DRIVEs, updated values, and broader identity. The cycle begins again, at a higher level.

### The Full Loop: Removing Willpower, Building Identity

The DRIVE Model works because it removes willpower from the equation. Instead of "trying harder," the client tracks signals, refines alignment, and lets identity generate momentum. Each cycle strengthens:

- **Resonance Ignition**: Values become embodied, not theoretical
- **Coherence Validation**: Body and mind align, reducing internal conflict
- **Propulsion**: Action becomes easier than resistance
- **Feedback Refinement**: Signals reveal what to keep, adjust, or release
- **Endurance Sustainability**: Identity carries the action forward
- **Evolution Expansion**: The system grows with the client

### Why This Model Works for Nervous System Rewiring

Traditional coaching asks: "What do you want to achieve?"  
The DRIVE Model asks: "What signals is your nervous system sending?"

By tracking the **triad** ‚Äî somatic, emotional, narrative ‚Äî before, during, and after each action, clients learn to distinguish:

- **Pre-activation residue** (inherited fear) from **true misalignment** (body wisdom)
- **Flow zone proof** (action feels easier than anticipation) from **forced action** (resistance persists)
- **Post-activation residue** (identity catching up) from **genuine misalignment** (persistent discomfort)

This creates a **diagnostic feedback loop** where every DRIVE becomes data, every signal becomes wisdom, and every action becomes identity.
