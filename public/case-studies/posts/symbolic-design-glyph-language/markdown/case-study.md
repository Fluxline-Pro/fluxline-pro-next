## Key Takeaways

### What Worked

1. **Archetypal Foundation**: Rooting glyphs in universal symbols created instant recognition
2. **Professional Execution**: Clean, modern design maintained credibility
3. **Teaching Integration**: Making glyphs pedagogical multiplied their value
4. **Iterative Development**: Testing with clients refined meaning and usage
5. **System Thinking**: Creating cohesive library enabled consistent application

### What We Learned

1. **Less is More**: Started with 20 glyphs, refined to 12 core symbols
2. **Semantic Clarity**: Each glyph needs clear, teachable meaning
3. **Context Matters**: Glyphs work best with supporting text/explanation
4. **Client Adoption**: Teaching glyph language creates engagement and retention
5. **Brand Equity**: Unique visual system becomes valuable IP

### Critical Success Factors

1. **Personal Investment**: Developed during evenings/weekends alongside full-time work
2. **Client Testing**: Early feedback shaped final glyph meanings
3. **Design Skill**: Ability to translate concepts into clean, scalable symbols
4. **System Integration**: Connecting glyphs to existing design system
5. **Business Alignment**: Ensuring glyphs served business and teaching goals
